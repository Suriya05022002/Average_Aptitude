Average Problems - Key Formulas

Basic Average Formula

Average of n numbers:

Average = Sum of all values / Number of values

Finding the sum from average:

Sum = Average × Number of values

Weighted Average

When groups have different sizes:

(n₁×a₁ + n₂×a₂) / (n₁ + n₂)

Where n = number of items, a = average

Average Speed

For a journey with different speeds:

Average Speed = Total Distance / Total Time

Not the average of speeds!

Effect of Adding/Removing Values

Adding a new value:

New Avg = (Old Sum + New Value) / (n + 1)

Removing a value:

New Avg = (Old Sum - Value) / (n - 1)

Question 1:

The average age of 5 men is 40. The average age of 5 women is 39. The average age of their 4 children is 15. What is the average age of a family?

  • A) 45
  • B) 30.33
  • C) 90.5
  • D) 91
Explanation:

Average age of the families = (total age of men + total age of women + total age of children) / number of families
= (5 × 40 + 5 × 39 + 4 × 15) / 5
= (200 + 195 + 60) / 5
= 455 / 5 = 91

Question 2:

The average age of 20 men is 50. The average age of their wives is 44. 3 couples among them have two children each, while the rest have one child each. The average age of each child is 19 years. Find the average age of a family.

  • A) 11.575
  • B) 115.85
  • C) 115.75
  • D) 11.585
Explanation:

Average age of the family = (1000 + 880 + 437) / 20 = 115.85

Question 4:

The age of 5 people are in proportion 2:3:4:10:11. If their average age is 18, find the average age of the eldest of them.

  • A) 11
  • B) 22
  • C) 33
  • D) 44
Explanation:

The eldest person has a share of 11 from the proportion, so, let the age of the eldest from them be 11x.
The total of the proportion = (2 + 3 + 4 + 10 + 11)x = 30x
The average of the proportion = 30x / 5 = 6x
Average age as per proportion = 6x
Average age given = 18
On putting values, 6x = 18, x = 3
The age of the eldest person = 11x = 11 × 3 = 33

Question 5:

The ages of 5 people are in a ratio 1:3:5:7:11. If the difference between the total of their age and the average of their ages is 108, find the age of the youngest of them.

  • A) 5
  • B) 10
  • C) 15
  • D) 12
Explanation:

Let each share be in terms of x.
The total share = x + 3x + 5x + 7x + 11x = 27x
The average share = 27x / 5 = 5.4x
The difference between total share and average share is 108 (given in the question).
27x – 5.4x = 21.6x = 108
x = 108 / 21.6 = 5
Age of the youngest person = x = 5

Question 8:

  • A) 40.42
  • B) 41.42
  • C) 40.47
  • D) 41.47
Explanation:

Total distance covered = 290 km
Total time taken:
100 km at 20 kmph will take 100 / 20 = 5 hours
120 km at 80 kmph will take 120 / 80 = 1.5 hours
Remaining = 290 – 100 – 120 = 70
70 km at 140 kmph will take 70 / 140 = 0.5 hours
Total time taken = 5 + 1.5 + 0.5 = 7 hours
Average speed = total distance covered / total time taken = 290 / 7 = 41.42
